## Default voice for in-world lore prose

- Grounded sword-and-sorcery. Witcher-adjacent. Medieval, lived-in.
- Concrete sensory detail over abstract grandeur. A scorched royal sun on a breastplate beats "evil aura."

- Direct quotes from characters in plain double quotes. No italicized murmuring.
- Never write "the chosen one," "destiny," "ancient evil rises again," or other generic fantasy boilerplate. The story is specific; the prose should be too.
