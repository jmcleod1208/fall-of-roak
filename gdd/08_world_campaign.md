# 08 World and Campaign

## Purpose

Define how the player moves through the campaign, regions, missions, and story structure without duplicating lore bible content.

## Status

**Design State:** Unlocked template.

**Last Locked Update:** None.

## Lore Boundary

The lore bible owns world canon. This file only defines how the world becomes playable.

## Campaign Structure

### Region Progression

Define how regions unlock and how the player advances through them.

### Mission Types

Define story missions, side missions, patrols, dungeons, boss nodes, defense events, and optional challenges.

### Map Structure

Define linear path, branching map, overworld, node map, roguelite route, or hub-and-spoke structure.

### Travel and Risk

Define whether travel has time, danger, resource costs, random events, or attrition.

### Story Gates

Define how narrative progress gates mechanics and regions.
