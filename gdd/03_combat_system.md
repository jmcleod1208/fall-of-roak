# 03 Combat System

## Purpose

Define the tactical combat rules that every battle follows.

## Status

**Design State:** Unlocked template.

**Last Locked Update:** None.

## Battle Structure

### Pre-Battle

Define scouting, unit selection, positioning, equipment, and preparation.

### Battle Start

Define how combat initializes.

### Round Flow

Define whether combat is turn-based, simultaneous, timed, phase-based, or hybrid.

### Action Resolution

Define attack order, ability casting, movement, targeting, and interrupts.

### Victory and Defeat

Define win conditions, loss conditions, draws, retreats, and surrender.

## Combat Rules

### Stats

Define all combat stats and formulas.

### Targeting

Define default targeting and special targeting rules.

### Damage and Healing

Define damage types, mitigation, shields, healing, resurrection, and death.

### Status Effects

Define buffs, debuffs, crowd control, damage over time, and cleanses.

### Randomness

Define critical hits, accuracy, dodge, proc rates, and deterministic alternatives.
