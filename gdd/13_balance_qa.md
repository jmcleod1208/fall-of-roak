## QA Checklist

- Battle starts correctly.
- Battle resolves correctly.
- Rewards are granted correctly.
- Save data updates correctly.
- UI communicates important state.
- Tooltips match mechanics.
- Locked lore names are spelled correctly.
- No mechanic contradicts locked lore constraints.
