# 02 Player Progression

## Purpose

Define how the player, party, and campaign grow over time.

## Status

**Design State:** Unlocked template.

**Last Locked Update:** None.

## Progression Tracks

### Party Progression

Define how heroes, units, or companions improve.

### Account or Save Progression

Define what is permanent across sessions.

### Campaign Progression

Define how regions, chapters, or story gates unlock.

### Difficulty Progression

Define how enemy strength, complexity, and pressure increase.

### Unlock Progression

Define when systems, units, items, modes, and features become available.

## Progression Rules

### XP and Leveling

Define whether XP exists, who earns it, and what levels change.

### Upgrades

Define upgrade types, costs, caps, and reset rules.

### Player Choice

Define where players make meaningful build or route choices.
