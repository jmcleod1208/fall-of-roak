# Fall of Ro'ak — Lore Bible

A grounded fantasy auto-battler RPG. Witcher-adjacent tone. Four heroes. One burning world. The Wanting Mother beneath it all.

The party of four — Runlan, Calypsa, Vallen Skye and Vander de la Cruz — walks east from the ashes of Fernfield, across the Old Forest, the fallen Demesne, and into Dreadvik, where the warlord Ro'ak waits, and the goddess who raised him waits beneath him.

## Key lore rules (do not break)

- **Dragons are real, native to Halig, Creator-made under Drakhalion the First Flame.** Exceedingly rare. Most sleep. Aetherion (Old Forest) is one. Vorthak is Noctheris's purpose-built exception, not a native dragon.
- **The Mothers wield no magic.** They are ordinary women who gave their unborn children to Noctheris. Necromancy and corruption acts are Noctheris reaching through her vessels (Ro'ak, Seraphiel), with the Mothers' offered grief as the cost.
- **House Valerian is fully extinct.** Vallen is a Skye, not Valerian blood. He becomes King of Crownhaven by founding a new dynasty.
- **Calendar:** AC (After Crowns) / BC (Before Crowns), anchored to Crownhaven's founding (Year 0). The present is Year 500 AC. The Hollowing was 1000 BC.
- **Five Old Gods:** Noctheris (Wanting Mother, primary villain), Visstheri (Thief), Aqueloth (Hullbreaker), Drakhalion (First Flame, dragons), Morhaem (Red Chalice, sequel villain).
